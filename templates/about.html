My Journey 🚀

I embarked on my tech voyage at GITAM University, where I graduated with a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering in 2023. The world of code first unfolded before me back in 10th grade, and it was love at first 'script'. Since then, I have been entwined in the endless loop of learning, experimenting, and evolving.

My professional canvas currently holds the vivid hues of an AI Intern, but my palette is heavily tinted with a keen interest in Full Stack Web Development. Python and JavaScript are my brushes of choice, and with Flask and Django, I've painted various web vistas and intricate APIs.

When I'm not busy translating logic into lines of code, you can find me diving into the captivating worlds of anime 🎥 or rhythmically tapping away to the beat of indie video games like Dead Cells, Celeste, and Shovel Knight 🎮.

Looking ahead, I yearn to contribute to the open-source community 🌐, believing that sharing knowledge propels us all forward. My dream is to script a career that's not just a series of jobs but a blend of experiences that leave a mark on the tech world.

Each problem to me is a puzzle 🧩 waiting to be solved, and I relish the process of deconstructing complexities to discover solutions. My approach is simple: understand the basics, break down the problem, and rebuild with a solution in mind.

That's a little window into my world 🌍. I'm excited to learn about yours, collaborate and create something impactful together!
